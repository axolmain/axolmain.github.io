Why pay for people when you can pay for food?

Why choose us?

Bread and Fish is an alternative to Door dash, UberEATS, Grub Hub, etc. with one key difference. Rather than charging users COVID-19 fees, absurd delivery fees, service charges, and additional fees that don’t need to be charged, we’ll only charge one fee because of our minimal costs and don’t require a tip because it’s a robot delivering food. You pay cheaper prices for the same products as competitors, it’s more environmentally friendly, and gives more student discounts. You can order food from the comfort of your own home, track the delivery without risking privacy of drivers, plus see a cool robot deliver food. Our prices include tax already so you don’t need to guess how much tax they pay until see the full price.

What's the benefit of switching?

With Bread and Fishyou can maximise your work/fun, by saving the time it takes to cook and clean up. The benefit of Bread and Fish is that we are specialized for BYU students and the campuses, offering special deals for students. The delivery method is also contactless but still brings the food right to your door because it’s via robots instead of people. It’s cheaper than alternatives regardless of the special deals due to the saved costs that come with using robots rather than people.
