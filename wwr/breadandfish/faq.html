FAQ

What's different about Fish and Bread?

Instead of using cars and peopple to deliver, we take a more environmental approach to food delivery and use solar panel powered robots. All assembled using recycled materials where possible. We work to make every part of the process as efficient and environmentally friendly as possible

How is it cheaper?

Typical food delivery companies use one app for ordering and one app for delivery drivers, but this alone costs more than just hosting one app. We only use one app as we don't offer the option to delivery, but because of this we don't have the cost of hosting an extra app or the need for paying drivers. We only charge a small delivery fee for maintenance and the rest comes from resturants and advertisement.

What resturants are offered here?

Being a service made by BYUI students, we offer any on-campus resturants delivery with complimentary delivery, and a wide variety of off-campus resturants. McDonalds, Wendys, Burger King, Panda Express and many more!

How are costs broken up?

We charge resturants 5% of each order and use non-invasive ads to get most of our revenue. Aside from this we charge a 20% up to $3 delivery fee as a base, but have many deals and days where this fee is either non existent, or only on certain items. If you purchase a monthly subscription, you receive exclusive deals PLUS don't need to pay any delivery fees!
