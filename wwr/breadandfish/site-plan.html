Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wondered why a $5 food delivery order costs $12? It’s because of all the fees that come with ordering food from human drivers. What if you could open a website with all the same options, order food, and have a robot deliver it right to your door without all those fees? Ordering from B&F would mean just that, you can enjoy the benefits of food delivery without worrying about breaking your bank account. With an interactive website and an easy-to-read interface you can log in and find your favorite meals straight from your couch and all you need to do it open the door and grab the food.

Audience

Do you order food but don't like all the fees? Have you needed to save money on groceries or going out just to satisfy your unquenchable thirst for 3am food delivery? College students already have so much debt to pay off, why waste money on useless things when you could have convieniency and save money? We look to help those wanting to save money with no expense to their quality of life.

Content

Home page

My site is an alternative to Door dash, UberEATS, Grub Hub, etc. with one key difference. Rather than charging users COVID-19 fees, absurd delivery fees, service charges, and additional fees that don’t need to be charged, we’ll only charge one fee because of our minimal costs and don’t require a tip because it’s a robot delivering food. They’ll come to pay cheaper prices for the same products as competitors, it’s more environmentally friendly, it’s connected with the BYUs to give students discounts and deals. They can order food from the comfort of their home, track the delivery without risking privacy of drivers, and it’s cool to see a robot deliver food. Also, the prices will be with tax included so people don’t need to guess how much tax they pay until see the full price.

Images for the Home page

What makes Bread & Fish© stand out?

Visitors will come to my site because it’s a site where they can actualize, they’re lazy potential, and save the time it takes to cook and utilize that for homework/work. The difference between my website and other food delivery websites is that my website will be specialized for BYU students and the campuses, offering special deals for students. The delivery method is also contactless but still brings the food right to your door because it’s via robots instead of people. It’s cheaper than alternatives regardless of the special deals due to the saved costs that come with using robots rather than people.

FAQ

Visitors will expect my site to answer any question the other delivery services answer, plus specific news regarding our partners. They will want to see the restaurants that use deliver using the robots, they want to see the full menu of said restaurants with consequent prices (tax included), and how long the delivery will take. They will want to see where their delivery currently is (and what stage of delivery it’s in) , and where they can order (a map with a radius or the opportunity for them to put their address in and the computer to say it’s okay or not).
